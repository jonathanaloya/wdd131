Site Plan: Wellness Hub

Site Name

Wellness Hub

This name represents a website dedicated to promoting physical activity and nutrition wellness. It aims to be a central resource for individuals seeking to improve their health through exercise and balanced diet recommendations.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Wellness Hub is to provide comprehensive resources on physical activity and nutrition. This includes workout routines, dietary plans, expert articles, and a community forum for users to share their experiences and advice.

Scenarios

  • What are some effective workout routines for beginners?
  • How can I plan a balanced diet that suits my lifestyle?

Color Schema

The selected color schema for Wellness Hub includes:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Used for headings, links, and buttons)
  • Secondary Color: #8BC34A (Used for background colors, accents, and hover effects)

Typography

The selected fonts for Wellness Hub are:

  • Heading Font: Lato
  • Body Font: Open Sans
